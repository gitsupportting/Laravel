My Courses

To start a course click on the yellow button labelled ‘Start Course’ below. You will then see a list of lessons contained within that course, after clicking Start on the first lesson you will be guided through each page in the course and be asked a number of questions related to the subject you are learning. At the end of the course you will see a score showing how well you have done. If you have any questions regarding the course please speak to your manager.
